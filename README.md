BOOKI - Online Library UX Case Study 📚✨

Booki is a high-fidelity mobile application design that aims to solve the problem of reading isolation and the difficulty of finding high-quality digital books. It transforms the act of reading into a social, engaging, and trackable journey.

🧐 The Problem
As a passionate reader, I identified several pain points:

Accessibility: Difficulty in finding specific titles or accessing high-quality digital versions.
Isolation: Reading is often a lonely activity, leading to loss of motivation.
Consistency: Many readers start books but fail to finish them due to a lack of social encouragement and visible progress.

💡 The Solution: BOOKI
Booki is not just a digital library; it's a Social Reading Ecosystem.

Digital Store: Access to high-quality, verified e-books.
The Squad (Reading Clubs): Users can join groups to read the same book simultaneously, share insights, and discuss chapters in real-time.
Gamified Progress: A dedicated board with charts and progress tracking to keep the momentum going.

🎨 Design System
Theme: Dark Mode (Optimized for long reading sessions).
Primary Color: Amber/Orange (Warm and energetic).
Typography: Modern Sans-serif (Inter/Roboto) for maximum readability.
